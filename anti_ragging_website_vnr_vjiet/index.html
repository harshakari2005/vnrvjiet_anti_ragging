Our Mission

The Anti-Ragging Committee at VNR VJIET is dedicated to eradicating ragging and promoting a culture of mutual respect, discipline, and compassion among students. We aim to create a secure and supportive campus where every student can thrive academically and personally without fear.

Our Vision

We envision a campus environment that is completely free of ragging in all its forms. Through awareness campaigns, strict regulations, and a responsive grievance system, we strive to build a community where all students feel empowered and safe.
